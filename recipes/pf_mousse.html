Passion Fruit Mousse

Description:

The Passion Fruit is known by being an citrus fruit, typically a tropical fruit. But besides her nature, this dessert is very sweet and a nice choice to finalize an meal. And it's very simple to do, you don't will need so many steps.

Ingredients:

  • 8 passion fruits;
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar;
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk;
  • 2 cups cream;

Steps to cook:

  1. STEP:

    2. Break passion fruits in half, and empty contents into a bowl. Use a little water to help rinse the juice out of the skins. Mix with hands to soften pulp. Strain through a sieve or cheesecloth. Stir in sugar and sweetened condensed milk.

  2. STEP:

    3. In a chilled bowl, beat cream until stiff peaks form. Fold 1/3 of the cream into the passion fruit mixture, then quickly fold in remaining cream until no streaks remain. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
